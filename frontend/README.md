To use this project, you need to sign in first. It is very simple, just like logging into a game or social media!

### 1. Sign Up (First Time User)
If you are new, you need to create an account:
- Go to the **Signup Page**.
- Enter your **Name**.
- Enter your **Email Address** (like `teacher@college.com`).
- Create a **Password** (keep it secret!).
- Click **Register**.

### 2. Login (Returning User)
If you already have an account:
- Go to the **Login Page**.
- Enter your **Email Address**.
- Enter your **Password**.
- Click **Login**.
